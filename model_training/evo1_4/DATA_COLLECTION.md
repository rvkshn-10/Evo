# Evo 1.4 route-head data collection

Evo 1.4 is a research model. Teacher-derived route labels prove feasibility but do not authorize production use.

Collect timestamped map-pin analyses with blocked headings/points, the route actually selected by safety staff, measured route-clear time, and the matching PeopleSense reading. Validate blueprint counts against official FCUSD egress plans and drills. Never upload restricted floor plans; use approved structured counts only.

Promotion requires held-out real drill rows across Folsom High, Vista del Lago, and Cordova Park. Until then, keep Evo 1.2 hybrid in production.
